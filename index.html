Why Compliance Matters in San Francisco

Legal Requirements

San Francisco has strict building codes and permit requirements. All construction projects must comply with city regulations to ensure safety and proper development standards.

Avoid Costly Delays

Non-compliant projects can face stop-work orders, fines up to $10,000+, and lengthy delays that cost more than proper permitting from the start.

Protect Your Investment

Proper compliance ensures your project adds value to your property and protects you from liability issues, insurance problems, and resale complications.
